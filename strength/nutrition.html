Our recipe of the day Lets get some ingredients for our delicious breakfast:

  1. Wholemeal bread
  2. 1 egg white
  3. 1 tsp Vanilla
  4. 2 tsp cinnamon
  5. 2/3 cup Almond milk

Instruccions:

Mix the egg, vanilla, cinnamon, and the milk together. Then, soak the bread in the mix for 10 seconds. Put it in a pan until is toasted from both sides.

TIPS:

Before you start your workout routine you must stretch it out and hydrate. Before your routine, please don't eat heavy. It is important to have a good sleep if you want to be in the process of being a healthy person and get where you want to be.
